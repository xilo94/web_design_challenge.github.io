Wind Speed(mph)

Due to low wind speed and mid-latitudes, regions near the equator experience much faster airflow and windspeed. This occurs because warm equatorial air rises higher into the atmosphere and migrates toward the poles. As a result, cooler and denser air moves over Earth's surface toward the Equator to replace the heated air.
